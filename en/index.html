El Cocora Cloud Forest

San Ramon, Alajuela, Costa Rica

Family-friendly ecotourism focused on nature: butterfly house, hummingbird observatory, trails (short and long), and botanical gardens. You will also find souvenirs, a restaurant/cafe, lodging, and guided or self-guided tours for birdwatching and photography.
